About us

At Felix Cursive Craft, we believe in the timeless elegance of cursive handwriting. Since our founding in 2003, we have been dedicated to nurturing the art of cursive writing, transforming it into a skill that resonates with both students and adults alike. Our journey began in a modest school in Andhra Pradesh, India, where our cursive handwriting training program received enthusiastic support from educators and students. This initial success laid the foundation for what would become a global endeavor.
Over the years, Felix Cursive Craft has expanded its reach beyond borders. From our roots in India to establishing a strong presence in Dubai, UAE, we have continuously refined our approach, focusing on personalized learning experiences that cater to each individual's unique style and pace. Our collaboration with IKEN EDUCATION, a leader in child skills development, has further propelled our mission to new heights, allowing us to bring our expertise to an international audience.
At Felix Cursive Craft, our expert instructors are committed to guiding you through every stroke, ensuring that you not only master the craft of cursive handwriting but also develop a style that is uniquely yours. As we continue to grow, our vision remains steadfast: to revive and preserve the beauty of cursive writing for generations to come, making it a cherished skill in an increasingly digital world.
Join us on this journey to bring the art of cursive handwriting to life, and discover the power of a beautifully crafted writing habit that stands the test of time.

Why is handwriting important?
Handwriting is an essential skill that enhances cognitive development, improves memory retention, and helps in fine motor skill development. It’s not just about writing words; it’s about developing a unique way to express ideas and communicate effectively.
How does handwriting help children and adults?
For children, handwriting improves hand-eye coordination, concentration, and reading and writing abilities. For adults, it enhances cognitive functions, aids in stress relief, and boosts creativity and memory.
What are the various types of handwriting?
The various types of handwriting include:
  • Print Handwriting The most basic form of handwriting, where each letter is written separately.
  • Cursive Writing: A flowing style where letters are connected, allowing for faster and more elegant writing.
  • Calligraphy: The art of beautiful writing, focusing on aesthetic form and style.
  • Others: Modern script, Italic handwriting, and various artistic styles.
What is the impact of handwriting on schooling and career?
Good handwriting can improve academic performance, as clear and legible writing is often associated with better grades. In the professional world, it can enhance your communication skills and make your written communication more impactful.
Is handwriting still required in the digital era?
Yes, despite the digital revolution, handwriting remains a crucial skill. It enhances cognitive development, improves focus, and is essential for note-taking, brainstorming, and personal expression. Studies also suggest that handwritten notes lead to better memory retention compared to typing.
Is cursive handwriting still relevant today?
Yes, cursive handwriting remains relevant for personal correspondence, signing documents, and in fields where clear, professional handwriting is valued. It also offers cognitive and creative benefits. It’s very much accepted globally in schools too.
What type of skills develop through handwriting?
Handwriting develops fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination, concentration, creativity, and cognitive abilities. It also fosters discipline and patience, as consistent practice is needed to improve.
What is the right age to start learning handwriting?
Children can start learning handwriting as early as 3-4 years old when they begin to develop fine motor skills. However, it’s never too late to learn or improve handwriting, making it suitable for all ages.
Is there a relationship between brain development and handwriting skills?
Yes, handwriting engages multiple areas of the brain simultaneously, including motor, cognitive, and memory regions. This integrated activity supports brain development and enhances learning and comprehension.
What skills can be enhanced or developed through handwriting?
Handwriting can enhance skills such as concentration, critical thinking, problem-solving, creativity, and organization. It also boosts self-discipline and personal expression.
How can I motivate my child to improve their handwriting?
Encourage them by making handwriting practice fun and engaging. Use colorful pens, interesting writing prompts, and reward progress. Demonstrating the importance of good handwriting in schoolwork and daily life can also motivate them.
How can I explain the importance of handwriting programs to parents?
Parents should understand that handwriting is more than just a school skill—it’s a crucial component of a child’s cognitive development. Programs like Felix Cursive Craft offer structured and enjoyable ways to improve handwriting, leading to better academic performance and lifelong benefits.
Is there a use case for adults?
Absolutely! Adults can benefit from handwriting courses to improve their personal and professional communication. Whether it’s refining their signature, taking better notes, or simply enjoying the art of calligraphy, handwriting skills can add a unique and personal touch to their written communication.
Why choose Felix Cursive Craft?
Felix Cursive Craft is your destination for overcoming all handwriting hurdles. Our courses are designed to cater to all age groups, helping you achieve legible, beautiful, and efficient handwriting that stands out in both academic and professional settings.
Do I need any prior experience with cursive writing?
No prior experience is needed. Our courses are designed for beginners as well as those who want to improve their existing cursive handwriting skills.
How long does it take to see improvement in my cursive handwriting?
The time it takes to see improvement depends on the individual’s starting point, the amount of practice, and the frequency of classes. Generally, noticeable progress can be seen within a few weeks of consistent practice.
What materials do I need for the class?
Basic materials include a notebook, pen, pencil, sharpener, eraser, and any hand-outs or worksheets provided by the instructor. Specific materials may vary depending on the course level.
How many students are in each class?
To ensure personalized attention, our classes are kept small, typically with a maximum of 1:8 students per session. This allows the instructor to cater to individual needs and pace.
What if I miss a class?
If you miss a class, you can refer to recorded sessions or request a brief recap from the instructor. It’s important to keep up with practice to ensure continued progress.
Is there an option to request one-on-one sessions?
Yes, we offer personalized one-on-one sessions tailored to your specific needs and goals. Please reach out to us for more details and to schedule a session.
Is there a family discount for multiple enrollments?
Yes, we offer family discounts for multiple enrollments in our courses. Please contact us for more details on our family discount packages.
